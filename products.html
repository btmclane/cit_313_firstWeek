Golf Tracker ProV1

About Golf Tracker ProV1

How does it track the ball?

Our balls are made with GPS chips inside of the ball that will show you where it is at to the exact location through our app which requires a subscription.

Other features

As well as keeping track of your ball, our app can also act as your typical GPS for seeing how far away from the green you are. You can also program the app to your exact club distances so it acts as your own personal caddie!

Pricing

On your first purchase, you will have to make an account which adds a $4.99 extra charge on top of the ball pricing. The balls come in packs of 6 for the price of $19.99, totalling your first purchase to $24.98. After you are subscribed the price is just for balls as needed!
